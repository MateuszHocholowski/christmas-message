As part of your Christmas gift, I've collected a few items that I hope you will find interesting and enjoyable:

1. The Cyberiad – This is a book by Poland's most acclaimed sci-fi/fantasy author, Stanisław Lem.

2. Neuroshima Hex – This is a board game that is very popular around here, so I figured it's unlikely you already have it in your arsenal. It's suitable for 2–4 players, fast-paced, and the gameplay is relatively short. I'm sure you'll enjoy playing it with the boys or some friends.

3. Scout Cross – This one, I hope, will pique your interest as you are deeply involved in scouting. The Scout Cross is a badge/award granted to Polish scouts who prove themselves worthy. Only after receiving this cross can one truly call themselves a scout and wear it on their uniform. It carries significant symbolism (I'm sure you can find articles about it if you're interested) and a rich history.

During WWII, Polish scouting was highly involved in the underground anti-occupation movement under the codename Grey Ranks, which gives this badge great meaning to Polish scouts today. This particular cross belonged to me, and I hope you'll find an opportunity to share its story with Scottish scouts, giving it a purpose beyond lying at the bottom of a closet.
